Bike Shopping Steps

How to Purchase a Bike

1

First, browse our selection of ready-to-ride bikes below. Use the size filters to make your search quick and easy!

2

Next, select your top 3 bikes to test ride. Note the make, model, size, and colour. You can try out additional bikes if time permits.

3

Ensure that the intended rider is available and present for the bike fitting.

4

Book an appointment. Please note that only one rider can be fitted per appointment. Test rides happen outside so please dress for the weather.

5

Please cancel your appointment if you cannot make it. No-shows without cancellation will receive lower priority and may be charged a booking fee for future appointments.

Keep Edmontonians Rolling

For over 40 years Bike Edmonton has led the charge to make Edmonton a place where cycling is not just possible but safer, easier, more fun, and more practical year round. Our work has supported funding for the Bike Plan, bike lane construction, and programs that give people resources they need to ride. We have helped thousands of people find a bike to ride and learn to fix their bike.

Your financial support makes it possible for us to do this work!
