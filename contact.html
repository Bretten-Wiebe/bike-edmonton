Keep Edmontonians Rolling

For over 40 years Bike Edmonton has led the charge to make Edmonton a place where cycling is not just possible but safer, easier, more fun, and more practical year round. Our work has supported funding for the Bike Plan, bike lane construction, and programs that give people resources they need to ride. We have helped thousands of people find a bike to ride and learn to fix their bike.

Your financial support makes it possible for us to do this work!
