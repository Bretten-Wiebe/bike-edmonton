About Us

Who Is BIKE Edmonton?

Established as the Edmonton Bicycle Commuters Society in 1980 and now known as Bike Edmonton, we sell refurbished bikes, help you fix your bike, teach you to ride, host #yegbike events, and promote accessible cycling for everyone.

Our Sponsors

A huge thank you to our incredible funders, sponsors, donors, and supporters who are committed to making Edmonton a more bike-friendly city!

Workshops

Need Repairs?

Bike Edmonton operates a fully-equipped, volunteer-run, community bike workshop. We have the tools (including specialty tools) needed to repair and maintain your bicycle, and mechanics to teach you how to do it.

Keep Edmontonians Rolling

For over 40 years Bike Edmonton has led the charge to make Edmonton a place where cycling is not just possible but safer, easier, more fun, and more practical year round. Our work has supported funding for the Bike Plan, bike lane construction, and programs that give people resources they need to ride. We have helped thousands of people find a bike to ride and learn to fix their bike.

Your financial support makes it possible for us to do this work!
